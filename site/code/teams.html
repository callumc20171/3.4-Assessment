OBHS Tournament Team

Coach: Brent Mateheare

The OBHS Tournament team compete every year to win the national tournament. Unfortunately due to Covid-19 regulations this year the Basketball NZ Secondary team national tournament was cancelled after the team qualified by beating Southland Boys' and Waitaki Boys' in the qualifying games.

In wake of the recent Covid restrictions, the tournament team plan to play in an upcoming Christchurch tournament featuring some of the South Islands best teams.
